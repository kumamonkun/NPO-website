About Us

Hearts Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to nourishing and empowering
homeless children by providing them with nutritious meals, fostering a supportive
community, and creating pathways to a brighter future. We aim to alleviate hunger and

break the cycle of homelessness among vulnerable children, ensuring they have the
opportunity to grow, thrive, and achieve their full potential..
